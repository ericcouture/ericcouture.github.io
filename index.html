Let's Make Propaganda

Workshop, Documentation Zine, Video
2020

Let's Make Propaganda was a project created as a "live art" piece. The goal was to embrace the art of teaching, and to attempt to leave an impact on the viewer.
As a part of this project, I also created a zine reflecting on the concept of art as propaganda, and on my experiences teaching the workshop. Photos graciously provided by Jack Lwin
